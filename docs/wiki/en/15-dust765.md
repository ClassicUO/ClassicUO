# Dust765

All options in the **Dust765** section of Modern Options, by left-panel subsection: Art/Hue, Visual Helpers, HealthBars, Cursor, Overhead, Old Healthlines, Misc, Misc2, Auto Loot, Buffbar UCC, Self, Macros, Gumps, Texture Manager, Lines, PvM/PvP.

---

## Art / Hue Changes

Visual art and color changes (stealth, energy bolt, gold, trees, blockers). Options: **Color stealth** (checkbox + color + neon combo), **Color Energy bolt** (checkbox + color + neon + art combo), **Change gold art to** (Normal/Cannonball/Prev Coin), **Change tree art to** (Normal/Stump/Tile), **Blocker Type** (Normal/Stump/Tile) with optional colors.

---

## Visual Helpers

Tile highlighting (range 1–20, color), spell-context variant, **Preview Fields**, **Preview Teleport Tiles**, **Color Own Aura By HP**, **Glowing Weapons** (Off/White/Pink/Ice/Fire/Custom), **Highlight Last Target** (same options), **Highlight Last Target Poisoned/Paralyzed** (combo + color).

---

## HealthBars (Dust765)

**Highlight LT Healthbar**, **Highlight Health Bar By State**, **Flashing Healthbar Outline** (Self/Party/Ally/Enemy/All), **Flashing Healthbar Negative Only**, **Only Flash On HP Change** (threshold 0–100).

---

## Cursor

**Show Spells On Cursor** (offset X/Y), **Color Game Cursor When Targeting**.

---

## Overhead / Old Healthlines

**Display Range In Overhead** (requires HP overhead). Old Healthlines: **Use Old Healthlines**, **Display Mana/Stam In Underline**, **Use Bigger Underlines**, **Transparency** (0–10).

---

## Misc (Dust765)

Offscreen targeting, set target out of range, override container range, close friend on world map, auto avoid obstacles, **Show Use/Loot modal when pressing Ctrl**, Razor target to lasttarget, **Outline statics black** (BROKEN), ignore stamina check, scale monsters, **Block Wall of Stone** / **Block Energy Field** (Fel only, Art ID, force AoS/hue).

---

## Misc2

**WireFrame** (BROKEN), **Hue impassable Tiles**, **Transparent/Invisible Houses and Items** (Z level, sliders), **Draw mobiles with surface overhead**, **Enable ignore list for circle of transparency**, **Show death location on world map for 5 min**, macros ToggleTransparentHouses / ToggleInvisibleHouses.

---

## Auto Loot / Buffbar UCC / Self

UCC Auto Loot (enable, grid coloring, loot above ID, delays, corpse colors). Buffbar (enable, swing/disarm lines, lock, cooldowns). Self automations (enable, pouches, cooldowns, thresholds, auto rearm, triggers).

---

## Macros (Dust765) / Gumps / Texture Manager / Lines

Special macros (LastTargetRC, ObjectInfo, HideX, HealOnHPChange, etc.). Gumps: LastTarget bar, Bandage gump, OnCasting gump, Mark Cave Tiles, Visual Response Manager. Texture Manager: halos and arrows (colors by notoriety). **Enable UCC - Lines**.

---

## PvM / PvP

Damage counter on last target (bar and/or overhead), aggro indicator, filter corpses by notoriety, low HP alert, kill count, highlight loot, criminal/attackable alert, war mode indicator, grey/criminal timer, highlight last attacker, spell range on cursor, quick-target enemy list, name overhead profiles (PvM vs PvP), configurable sounds, block beneficial spells on enemies.
